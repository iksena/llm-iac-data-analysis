- **Log Group Name** - Where VPC Flow Logs are stored

## 5. CloudWatch Logs Insights Queries Design

The four required widgets will use these queries:

### Widget 1: Top Source IPs by Outbound Traffic
```sql
filter interfaceId like 'eni-' and destinationPort != 22
| stats sum(bytes) as bytes by srcAddr
| sort bytes desc
| limit 10
```

### Widget 2: Top Source IPs by Rejected Connections
```sql
filter interfaceId like 'eni-' and action = 'REJECT'
| stats count(*) as rejectedConnections by srcAddr
| sort rejectedConnections desc
| limit 10
```

### Widget 3: Top Destination IPs by Outbound Traffic
```sql
filter interfaceId like 'eni-' and destinationPort != 22
| stats sum(bytes) as bytes by dstAddr
| sort bytes desc
| limit 10
```

### Widget 4: Traffic Volume Over Time
```sql
filter interfaceId like 'eni-' and destinationPort != 22
| stats sum(bytes) as bytes by bin(1h)
| sort bin asc
```
